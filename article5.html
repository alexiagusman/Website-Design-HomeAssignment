Makeup

By Mary

The ancient Egyptians are credited with pioneering the use of makeup around 4000 BCE, using minerals like copper and lead for eye cosmetics.

Makeup has a rich and diverse history that spans across different cultures and centuries. Ancient civilizations such as the Egyptians, Greeks, and Romans all played significant roles in shaping the use of cosmetics.

Throughout history, makeup trends evolved, influenced by cultural shifts and societal norms. In the Middle Ages, a fair complexion was highly prized, and women used substances like lead-based powders to achieve a pale look. The Renaissance period saw a return to more natural aesthetics, with an emphasis on clear skin and delicate features.
