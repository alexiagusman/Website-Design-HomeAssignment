Swimming

By Jane

Swimming is a full-body workout that provides various health benefits.

Swimming is a low-impact exercise, making it gentle on joints and suitable for people of all ages and fitness levels. The buoyancy of water reduces the impact on bones and joints, making it an ideal exercise for those with arthritis, joint pain, or mobility issues. Additionally, swimming helps improve cardiovascular health, muscle strength, flexibility, and overall endurance. .

Swimming has been a part of human history for a very long time. The first known references to swimming as a recreational activity date back to ancient Egypt around 2500 BCE.
